# Privacy Policy — lattttice

**Effective date:** 2026-06-23

**lattttice does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data.**

- The app runs entirely on your device.
- No network requests are made.
- No analytics, no crash reporting, no advertising SDKs.
- Game state and settings are saved on your device using the operating system's standard local storage. They never leave the device.
- No account, email, phone number, or identifier is requested or stored.
- No camera, microphone, location, contacts, photo library, or other system permissions are requested.

If you have questions, contact the developer at **raban.heller@outlook.com**.

This policy will be hosted at a public URL before submission (see SUBMISSION-lattttice.md → §4).
